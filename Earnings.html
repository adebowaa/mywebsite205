Champions League Financing

After research I was able to determine how much a club earns from their team participating in the champions league. I have done this by comparing the earning of last years finalists in the champions league, which were Barcelona and Juventus.

Stage FC Barcelona Juventus
Participation €8.6 million €8.6 million
Performance bonus €5 million(for winning 5 group games) €3.5 million ( for winning 3 group games + 1 draw)
Market Pool €24.6 million €58.2 million
Round of the 16 €3.5 million €3.5 million
Quarter Finals €3.9 million €3.9 million
Semi Finals €4.9 million €4.9 million
Finals €10.5 million (winners) €6.5 million (runners up)
